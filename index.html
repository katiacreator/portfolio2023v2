About

Hello World! I am a certified full-stack software engineer. As a proud graduate of Temple University, my academic background is in medical sociology, specifically secondary data analysis of existing CDC data.

I have over 10 years of professional experience providing case management services and database management for various nonprofits. I am interested in the concept of futuristic archives and have a long-term goal of using code to become a virtual museum curator.
